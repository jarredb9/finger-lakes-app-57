# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Tone:** Friendly and inviting. The application should feel like a helpful companion on a winery tour.
- **Voice:** Community-focused and warm. Use inclusive language that encourages exploration and sharing.

## Branding & Aesthetic
- **Visual Style:** Modern & Minimalist. Focus on clean lines, ample whitespace, and high-quality imagery to showcase wineries.
- **Consistency:** Maintain a polished and professional appearance while remaining approachable.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Mobile-First:** Prioritize responsive design and seamless touch interactions, ensuring a great experience on the go.
- **Accessibility-Focused:** Ensure WCAG compliance, high contrast, and robust keyboard navigation for all users.
- **Zero-Latency Feel:** Leverage optimistic updates and efficient data fetching to provide a snappy, responsive interface.

## Error Handling & Feedback
- **Communication:** Helpful & Instructional. When errors occur, clearly explain what happened and provide actionable steps to resolve the issue.
- **Feedback Loops:** Use toasts and alerts to keep users informed of their actions and system status in real-time.
